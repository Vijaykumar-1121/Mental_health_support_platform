A Space for Student Wellness

Navigating student life is a unique journey with its own set of pressures. MindWell was born from the understanding that mental health is a crucial part of that journey. We're not just a platform; we're a companion dedicated to providing the tools and support you need to thrive, not just survive.

Built with Empathy

Created by a student, for students. We get it. Our tools and resources are designed with your specific challenges in mind.

Always Accessible

Support shouldn't have office hours. Access our AI assistant, resources, and tools 24/7, whenever you need them.

Confidential & Safe

Your privacy is paramount. We provide a secure and non-judgmental space for you to explore your thoughts and feelings.

The Story Behind MindWell

"As a student myself, I saw my peers and I struggling with the weight of academic pressure, social anxiety, and the general stress of university life. I realized there was a need for a centralized, student-focused platform that offered immediate, judgment-free support. That's why I created MindWell — to be the resource I wish I had. It’s a place to find clarity, connect with others, and remember that you're not alone."

Uppu Vijaykumar

SRMAP Student | Web Developer
